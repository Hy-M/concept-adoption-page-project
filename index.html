About our cats

Our cats and kittens are currently rehomed at our sanctuary in Batley, West Yorkshire. But there are only so many cats we can house, and each one of them deserves a loving, nurturing human to call their own

Many of our cats have heart-breaking stories, from being neglected and abused, to abandonment.

That's why we have taken it upon ourselves to help our furry feline friends experience love again in new homes, and we need your help to do that.

All of our cats are:

  • Vaccinated
  • Treated against fleas
  • Neutered
  • Micro-chipped
